Zen Koan Trivia Game

Koans come from the Zen tradition of Mahayana Buddhism. They are paradoxical, non-logical riddles which aim at the surrender and dissolution of categorical thinking. The ultimate goal is to gain a deeper inside into the nature of reality itself, and students of the Zen tradition may sit for weeks and even years on end contemplating a single koan. Some of the koans shared here are legitimate and some are mere fabrications... In either case, don't expect to understand these, just have fun with them.
